About the Shujaa Scholarship Management System

This system is designed to help New Hope Initiative efficiently manage and visualize student sponsorship data for the Shujaa Scholarship Programme. It provides real-time insights into the number of sponsored and unsponsored students, as well as the distribution of sponsorships across different churches.

Pie Chart: The pie chart below shows the percentage of students who are currently sponsored versus those who are not. This gives a quick overview of the programme's sponsorship coverage.
Bar Graph: The bar graph displays the number of sponsored students per church, ordered from the lowest to the highest. This helps identify which churches are most and least involved in supporting students.
